Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is n8n automation?

n8n is a powerful, flexible workflow automation tool we use to connect different software applications (like your CRM, Google Sheets, email, etc.) and automate repetitive tasks. This saves your business valuable time and resources, allowing your team to focus on growth.

How can AI Consulting benefit my business?

Our AI consulting helps you identify opportunities to leverage artificial intelligence for efficiency, generating insights, and driving innovation. We provide tailored strategies, from effective prompt engineering for LLMs to implementing practical AI solutions, designed to give you a significant competitive edge.

Is Roger Malek AI Agency based only in Manchester?

While we are proudly based in Manchester, UK, we offer our AI automation and consulting services to clients globally. We leverage modern remote collaboration tools effectively to work with businesses anywhere in the world.
